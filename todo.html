Welcome to Puerto Rico

Different places to visit

El Yunque

It's the only tropical rain forest in the national forest system. In it there are located hiking trails and a couple of waterfalls where you can bathe in really cold tropical water, one of the most commonly known is La Mina.

San Juan

El Viejo San Juan, the oldest city on US territory. La perla, one of the most unique neighborhoods and very rich in history, located between San Cristobal fort and El Morro, two of the most historical landmarks. Close by you also have the San Juan port.

Vieques

Remote island 16 miles from the east coast of Puerto Rico, very well know for discovering and snorkeling. Incredibly pretty. One of the most common known places is the Bahia bioluminescent (Mosquito bay), $2/adults; kids the age of 3-11 $1 and adults 60-74; also, after the age of 75 is free.

Culebra

Remote island 17 miles from the east coast of Puerto Rico. It is home for one of the most beautiful beaches I have been; Flamenco Beach, with very shallow and clear waters with a turquois color. $2.25/adults; kids the age of 3-11 is $1 and adults 60-74; also, age of 75 and upwards is free.
